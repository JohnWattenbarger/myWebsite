About Me

Hi I'm John Wattenbarger, a software developer currently working at NASA to develop new software for the Orion spacecraft as part of the Artemis mission. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of St. Thomas Houston. While in college, I founded the Computer Science Club to help teach students more about programming, and to team up with other Computer Science students. I served as president of the Computer Science Club for 2 years, where I collaborated with officers and other school clubs to host multiple fun and informative events. After graduating in December of 2018, I began working as a software developer at NASA.

While at NASA, I have worked to develop 3 new applications frequently switching between frontend and backend programming. I pride myself on being a flexible developer that is able to pick up new languages and frameworks quickly while maintaining quality, maintainable code. I created this website while in college to teach myself the basics of web development. This website contains some information about me, and show off some simple JavaScript games I created to improve my coding skills.
